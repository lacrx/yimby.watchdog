# California Housing Policy — Major Sources & Feeds

## State Agencies (Primary Authority)

| Source | What It Produces | Feed |
|--------|-----------------|------|
| **HCD** | Housing Element reviews, RHNA allocations, Technical Assistance letters, enforcement actions, APR data, MPO advisories | [APR Dashboard](https://www.hcd.ca.gov/housing-open-data-tools/apr-dashboard) · [Open Data (CSV/API)](https://data.ca.gov/dataset/housing-element-annual-progress-report-apr-data-by-jurisdiction-and-year) · Email list signup at hcd.ca.gov |
| **Attorney General** | Civil penalties, NOVs, legal opinions (Builder's Remedy), enforcement suits | [Press releases](https://oag.ca.gov/media/news) · [Housing enforcement](https://oag.ca.gov/housing) |
| **California Coastal Commission** | LCP certifications, staff reports, modification orders, coastal development permits | [Meeting agendas](https://www.coastal.ca.gov/meetings/agenda/) · [Current agenda](https://www.coastal.ca.gov/mtgcurr.html) · [Archive](https://www.coastal.ca.gov/meetings/archive/) |
| **OPR / LCI** | CEQA guidelines, General Plan guidelines | [CEQA updates](https://www.lci.ca.gov/ceqa/) · [Technical advisories](https://lci.ca.gov/ceqa/technical-advisories.html) · Email list at lci.ca.gov |
| **LAO** | Fiscal analyses of housing bills, policy briefs | [RSS feeds](https://lao.ca.gov/RSS) · [Publications](https://lao.ca.gov/publications) |
